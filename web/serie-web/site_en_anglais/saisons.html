Season 1

A mysterious man, nicknamed the Professor (El Profesor), plans the best robbery ever organized. To execute his plan, he recruits the best criminals in the country who have nothing to lose: Tokyo, Nairobi, Rio, Moscow, Berlin, Denver, Helsinki and Oslo.
The goal is to infiltrate the National Currency and Stamp Factory to print 2.4 billion euros, in less than eleven days and without shedding a drop of blood. Still, the group will be responsible for 67 hostages including Alison Parker, the daughter of the UK ambassador.
